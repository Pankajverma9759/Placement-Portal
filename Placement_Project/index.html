About Us

Welcome to the Placement Cell of our institution. Our mission is to bridge the gap between students and the corporate world by providing excellent career opportunities. We strive to prepare students with the necessary skills and knowledge required to excel in their professional lives.

With strong collaborations with top companies and organizations, we ensure that our students get the best placements and internship opportunities. Our dedicated team continuously works to bring in new recruiters and conduct placement drives, mock interviews, resume-building sessions, and skill development workshops.

We believe in empowering students with industry-oriented training and guidance, helping them achieve their career aspirations.
